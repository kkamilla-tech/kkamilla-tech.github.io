Kamilla

Fine Artist & Theatre Maker

Kamilla is a multidisciplinary artist working at the intersection of fine art, performance, and film. Her practice spans drawing, painting, sculpture, and scenic design, with a particular interest in the texture of materials and the narrative potential of space. She has exhibited internationally and collaborated with theatre companies across Europe.

"I am drawn to the quiet weight of things — the way light falls on a surface, the way a stage holds silence. My work is an attempt to make that stillness visible."
